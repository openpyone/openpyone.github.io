Logic Puzzle

Can You Figure Out What Subject Miss Apple Teaches?

There are three students — Banana, Cherry, and Durian — talking to the new transfer student, Watermelon, about what subject Miss Apple teaches.

Banana: "Miss Apple teaches English."

Cherry: "Miss Apple doesn't teach Science."

Durian: "Miss Apple either teaches English or Math."

After hearing all that, Watermelon is still confused. So he goes straight to ask the teacher.

Watermelon: "Excuse me, Miss Apple, what subject do you actually teach?"

Miss Apple: "Some of what your classmates said is true, and some isn't. Think about it!"

Question:

Based on what everyone said, what subject does Miss Apple really teach?
